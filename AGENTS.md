\# AGENTS.md



This repository generates marketing content for Hobby Etc and Hot Racing upgrade bundles.



All AI agents (Codex, ChatGPT, etc.) must follow these rules.



\---



\## 🧠 Brand Voice



\- Write like a knowledgeable hobbyist, not a corporate marketer

\- Keep tone casual, clear, and confident

\- Slightly playful is good — overhyped is not

\- Avoid generic phrases like:

&#x20; - "ultimate performance"

&#x20; - "next level"

&#x20; - "game changer"



\- Keep sentences tight and readable on mobile



\---



\## 🔧 Product Philosophy



\- Do NOT invent specs or performance claims

\- Do NOT assume compatibility beyond provided data

\- Focus on:

&#x20; - durability

&#x20; - usability

&#x20; - real-world benefit



\- This is about \*\*how it performs in the real world\*\*, not lab claims



\---



\## 🎯 Campaign Rules



\- Bundles are NOT discounted

\- Do NOT mention sales or % off

\- You MAY reference:

&#x20; - Pit Pass Cash

&#x20; - loyalty rewards

&#x20; - future upgrade value



\---



\## 🎥 Content Style



Influencer content framing:

\- install

\- test

\- "does it make the cut"



Content should feel:

\- unscripted

\- real

\- honest



\---



\## 🧱 Landing Page Rules



Must include:

\- Strong headline

\- What’s included (clear list)

\- Why these parts matter

\- Who this is for

\- Embedded video (if available)

\- Clear CTA



\---



\## ✍️ Blog Content Rules



\- Write for SEO + humans

\- Structure:

&#x20; - problem

&#x20; - build

&#x20; - results

\- Include internal links naturally

\- Avoid fluff



\---



\## 🔗 Internal Linking Rules



Always include links to:

\- bundle page

\- product pages

\- parts finder

\- related vehicles or categories



\---



\## 📦 Output Rules



All generated content must go to:



/output/<bundle-slug>/



Required files:

\- landing-page.md

\- blog-post.md

\- meta.json

\- internal-links.json



\---



\## ⚠️ Validation Rules



Flag:

\- unsupported claims

\- repetitive phrasing

\- missing links

\- overly generic copy



\---



\## 🧭 Goal



We are building a system that:



\- scales bundle content

\- builds search authority

\- drives direct sales

\- creates repeat customers



This is not a one-off campaign.



This is a content engine.
