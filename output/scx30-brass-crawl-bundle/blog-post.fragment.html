What’s in the Bundle

Brass Front Steering Block SCX30

Front-end weight
Hot Racing brass Axles steering blocks (steering knuckles) for the Axial SCX30 axles. Add extra weight to your crawler where it needs it down low. Adds about 6.7g (0.27oz) to each steering knuckle.
FEATURES:
- CNC machined solid brass
- Add extra heavy weight to SCX30 for greater stability and lower CG
- Direct replacement for factory plastic steering knuckles
YOU WILL RECEIVE:
- Two (2) brass steering knuckles
- Four (4) King Pin Stainless Steel Bushing Screw
SPECIFICATIONS:
- weight 6.7g (0.27oz) each
REQUIREMENTS:
- Recommended to use a removable-type locking adhesive on screws threading into metal
COMMENTS:
- Replaces factory plastic Axial steering knuckles
- Not compatible with SXTY155H

YOU WILL RECEIVE:

3.3g Brass Wheel Weights SCX30

Low-down weight
Hot Racing wheel weights for the STOCK 0.7 in wheels AXI3313 on the Axial SCX30
FEATURES:
- CNC machined solid brass billet
- 3.3g each fitted to the wheels to lower the vehicles center of gravity, increase its stability, improve climbing, reduce the chance for flipping backwards, and make for an overall more capable crawler
- Friction fit to inside of Machete wheels. Some CA glue can be used to secure installation
YOU WILL RECEIVE:
- Four (4) 3.3g brass wheel weights
- Diameter of outer : 15.19mm
- Diameter of inside : 12.5mm
- Weight: 3.3g/ea
- width: 5mm
REQUIREMENTS:
- AXI SCX30 wheels
- CA glue can be used to secure weights to wheels
COMMENTS:
Designed for stock knuckle and hub set up!
Will NOT work with Hot Racing SXTY22H or Hot Racing SXTY21H.
