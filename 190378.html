Description
Hot Racing brass Axles steering blocks (steering knuckles) for the Axial SCX30 axles. Add extra weight to your crawler where it needs it down low. Adds about 6.7g (0.27oz) to each steering knuckle.
FEATURES:
- CNC machined solid brass
- Add extra heavy weight to SCX30 for greater stability and lower CG
- Direct replacement for factory plastic steering knuckles
YOU WILL RECEIVE:
- Two (2) brass steering knuckles
- Four (4) King Pin Stainless Steel Bushing Screw
SPECIFICATIONS:
- weight 6.7g (0.27oz) each
REQUIREMENTS:
- Recommended to use a removable-type locking adhesive on screws threading into metal
COMMENTS:
- Replaces factory plastic Axial steering knuckles
- Not compatible with SXTY155H
